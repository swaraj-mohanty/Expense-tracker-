Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 7, 2026

1. Data We Collect

When you create an account, we collect your name and email address. When you use Spendly, we collect the expense data you enter, including amounts, categories, dates, and descriptions. We do not collect payment information or sensitive financial credentials.

2. How We Use Your Data

Your data is used solely to provide and improve the Spendly service. This includes displaying your expense history, generating summaries, and filtering by date or category. We do not use your data for advertising or sell it to third parties.

3. Data Storage

Your data is stored securely on our servers. We take reasonable technical measures to protect it from unauthorised access, loss, or misuse. However, no system is completely secure, and we encourage you to use a strong, unique password for your account.

4. Third Party Services

Spendly uses Google Fonts to load typefaces. This may result in your browser making a request to Google's servers. We do not integrate with any third-party analytics, advertising, or data-sharing services.

5. Contact Us

If you have questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy or how your data is handled, please reach out via the contact information provided on our website. We will respond as promptly as possible.
